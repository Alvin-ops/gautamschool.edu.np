About Our Founder/Principal

                  “DO GOOD TO ALL”
Inspired by the teachings of Gautama Siddhartha (BUDDHA), Mrs. Urmila Lama founded GAUTAM SCHOOL in 2033 BS, with just four children. Mrs Lama kept two children on her lap and two beside her and a small handmade blackboard in a small room. And there was no looking back. 45 years from then Gautam School is a pre-kindergarten through tenth-grade private independent day & boarding school for girls and boys over 2000 plus pupils in Birgunj, Nepal. Mrs Lama’s fortitude and struggle to build this institution is beyond the measure of any. Whether it is in the classroom, on the field, or in the community, every minute of every Gautam School day helps students to grow to become principled young citizens.
Poverty is not a criterion for selection: give quality education to those capable of receiving it, whether rich or poor.” - Mrs Urmila Lama (Founder/Principal)
